Title: E-commerce Sales Dashboard.
Business problems :
1. Tracking overall online sales performance in one place.
2. Identify top-performing products, regions, and customer segments.
3. Analyzing Sales trend overtime.
4. Understanding profit contribution and loss making areas.

 Tools and Technology used : 
 1. Power BI desktop
 2. power query
 3. Data modeling
 4. DAX

Key Insights:
1. Identified top 5 revenue generating products
2. Discovered highest sales coming from specific states
3. Found seasonal sales trend (peak months and low performing months)
4. Detected categories with high sales but low profit margin.
